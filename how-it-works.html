How Chatbot Smith Works

From first call to live chatbot in about a week. Here's exactly what to expect.

The process, step by step.

01

We get to know your business.

After your free 20–30 minute intro call, we schedule a setup call — about 45 minutes — where we ask about your services, your pricing, how you currently handle customer questions, and what you wish you didn't have to deal with. You don't need to prepare anything. Just show up.

→ A clear picture of what your chatbot will do before we build anything.

We build it — you don't have to.

After the call, we configure your chatbot from scratch. We write the responses, set up the booking flow, and match it to your brand voice. You're not filling out forms or choosing from templates — we do the work.

→ A fully built chatbot, tested and ready for your review.

We'll walk you through the finished chatbot together. If anything needs tweaking — a response that's off, a service you forgot to mention — we fix it on the spot. Once you're happy, we install it on your site and go live.

→ A live chatbot on your website, usually within a week of your first call.
